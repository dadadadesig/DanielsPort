User Personas

Understanding our users to tailor the app to their needs and preferences.

Busy Commuter

  • Goals: Quick parking, low cost, easy payment.
  • Frustrations: Traffic jams, complex apps, high prices.
  • Motivation: Arrive at work on time without stress.

Occasional Driver

  • Goals: Simple sign-up, occasional use.
  • Frustrations: Too many steps, advanced settings.
  • Motivation: Hassle-free parking when needed.
